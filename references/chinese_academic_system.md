# Chinese Academic System Reference

Quick reference for the Chinese university hierarchy, talent programs, and funding landscape. Essential context for evaluating advisors at Chinese institutions.

## Academic Ranks (职称体系)

| Chinese | English | Typical Requirements |
|---------|---------|---------------------|
| 讲师 | Lecturer | PhD + some publications |
| 副教授 | Associate Professor | 5-8 years experience, NSFC grants, h-index 8+ |
| 教授 | Full Professor | 10+ years, multiple NSFC grants, h-index 15+ |
| 特聘教授 | Distinguished/Chair Professor | Major talent title, h-index 25+ |
| 青年研究员 | Young Researcher/Assistant Professor (tenure-track) | Competitive hire, 6-year tenure clock |

## Major Talent Programs (人才项目)

Listed roughly in order of prestige:

| Program | Chinese Name | Age Limit | Annual Stipend | Significance |
|---------|-------------|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| CAS/CAE Academician | 院士 | None | Varies | Highest academic honor in China |
| Changjiang Distinguished Prof | 长江学者特聘教授 | <55 | ~200K RMB | Top-tier national recognition |
| NSFC Distinguished Young Scholar | 国家杰出青年科学基金（杰青） | <45 | 4M RMB grant | Gold standard for mid-career |
| Ten Thousand Talents (Leading) | 万人计划领军人才 | <55 | 1M RMB | National talent plan |
| NSFC Excellent Young Scholar | 优秀青年科学基金（优青） | <38(M)/<40(F) | 2M RMB grant | Key milestone for young PI |
| Changjiang Young Scholar | 长江学者青年学者 | <40 | ~100K RMB | Young talent recognition |
| "Four Youth" talents | 四青人才 | <35-40 | Varies | Collective term for young talent programs |

### Interpretation Guide

- **Has 杰青**: Top-tier PI, strong funding, large lab, high student competition
- **Has 优青**: Rising star, well-funded, good for ambitious students
- **Has neither**: May be early career, or a solid but not "star" researcher
- **Absence ≠ weakness**: Many excellent advisors lack these titles due to quota limits

## Funding Programs (科研基金)

### National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC / 国家自然科学基金)

| Type | Chinese | Typical Amount | Duration | Significance |
|------|---------|---------------|----------|-------------|
| Young Scientist | 青年基金 | 200-300K RMB | 3 years | Entry-level independent grant |
| General Program | 面上项目 | 500-800K RMB | 4 years | Standard PI-level grant |
| Key Program | 重点项目 | 2-3M RMB | 5 years | Major program, high prestige |
| Major Program | 重大项目 | 10M+ RMB | 5 years | Large team, national priority |
| Distinguished Young | 杰青 | 4M RMB | 5 years | Also serves as talent title |
| Excellent Young | 优青 | 2M RMB | 3 years | Also serves as talent title |

### Other Funding Sources

| Source | Typical Amount | Notes |
|--------|---------------|-------|
| Ministry of Science and Technology (科技部) | Varies | 973, 863, National Key R&D |
| Provincial NSFC equivalent | 100-500K RMB | Shanghai, Jiangsu, etc. |
| University internal grants | 100-300K RMB | Startup, cultivation funds |
| Industry/hospital collaboration | Varies | May indicate translational potential |

### Funding Assessment Guide

- **Continuous NSFC funding** (no gaps): Strong signal of sustained research quality
- **Escalating grant size**: PI is gaining momentum
- **Funding gaps >3 years**: Potential red flag for research inactivity
- **Only university internal grants**: May indicate difficulty securing competitive funding
